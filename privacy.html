Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Data Storage

EscapeMint stores all your data locally on your device. Your portfolio data, fund configurations, entries, and settings are saved as local files that never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export them or enable iCloud sync.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your portfolio data is stored in your personal iCloud Documents container. This data is encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest. We do not have access to your iCloud data. For information about how Apple handles iCloud data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

EscapeMint does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Financial account credentials or brokerage login information
  • Trade execution data sent to any third party

Biometric Authentication

EscapeMint supports Face ID and Touch ID to protect your portfolio data. Biometric data is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. We never access, store, or transmit biometric data.

In-App Purchases

EscapeMint may offer optional features via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. For information about how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

EscapeMint does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts in the app.

No Account Required

EscapeMint does not require any account creation, login, or authentication beyond optional device biometrics.

Data Export

You can export your data as a backup JSON file at any time from the app's Settings. This file is stored locally and shared only through methods you choose (AirDrop, Files, etc.).

Children's Privacy

EscapeMint does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no social features, user-generated content sharing, or external communication mechanisms.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing funds in the app or uninstalling it. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove data from iCloud via your device's Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at escapemint@shadowpuppet.net.
